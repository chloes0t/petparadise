Cats, Cookies, & Coffee

Sunday, July 28th from 3 to 4pm

Do you love animals? Would you like to volunteer locally but feel you don't have enough time? Then CPAW NJ is the perfect local animal charity for you!

Join us here at Brookdale Pet Center for free coffee and cookies and find out how you can help save our local homeless cats! Karen from CPAW will be here to lead the discussion and recruit volunteer. There are many opportunities and volunteer roles available that you can do on your own schedule!
